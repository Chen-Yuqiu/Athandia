Edit Your Personal Info


1.Account Setting

Hopefully, you have already used your e-mail address to sign up.

After clicking 'log in', you will open your usermain page.

First of all, you need to set your account, Click 'Account Setting', then Click'edit Account' and Edit your account info.

Return back to 'My Projects', Click 'Click Here to Add New Projects' and Edit your first projects details.

After click "Submit", now you have your first project in your dashboard. Click "edit project" to open certain project. Click "delete project" to delete certain one.

To see how to further edit your own project, click the "Edit Projects" on the guide menu.
